FAQ

Common Questions

Is there really just one plan?
Yes. $129/month, everything included. We don't believe in tiers that lock you out of features you need to run your business. You get the full platform from day one.
What happens after the 14-day free trial?
You'll be prompted to enter a card and continue for $129/month. If you decide not to continue, your account simply stops — no charges, no cancellation fees. We don't auto-charge without you knowing it's coming.
Do I need a credit card to start the trial?
No. Create your account and get full access for 14 days with no card required. You only enter payment info if you decide to keep going after the trial.
How many users can I add?
Unlimited. Add every tech, office staff member, and manager at no extra cost. No per-user fees. Ever.
What's the SMS pricing?
500 outbound SMS messages per month are included in your $129. If you go over, you can add blocks of 500 for $15 each. Most businesses with under 300 active properties never hit the 500 limit.
Are there any contracts or annual commitments?
None. Month to month, always. Cancel anytime from your account settings.
Does this work for both lawn care and pest control?
Yes. IndustryBossPro was built for spray businesses — lawn care, pest control, mosquito treatment, or any combination. The service types, chemical tracking, and waiting list work the same way regardless of what you spray.
What if I have questions or need help getting set up?
We're a small team and we respond fast. Reach us anytime at support@industrybosspro.com. We've run routes ourselves — we know what setup actually looks like and we're here to help.
