 iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1

2

3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — the app icon will appear on your home screen
🦋 Android (Chrome)
1
Open the link above in Chrome
2
Tap the 3 dots menu in the top right corner
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add
5
If you don't see it on your home screen, swipe up to open your app drawer and look for it there. You can long press the icon and drag it to your home screen.
💡 Tip: Once added to your home screen, tap the icon to open the app directly — no browser bar, just like a native app.
💡 Why we do it this way: Every time we push an update to the App Store it takes 1–2 weeks to be approved. By installing it this way, you always get our latest updates instantly — no waiting.
